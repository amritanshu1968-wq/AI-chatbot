Data Protection Principles

Our commitment to your privacy and data security

End-to-End Encryption

All conversations are encrypted using AES-256 encryption. Your data is protected in transit and at rest with military-grade security protocols.

  • AES-256 encryption standard
  • TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Zero-knowledge architecture

Privacy Protection

Your conversations are never used for training AI models without explicit consent. We maintain strict data isolation and user privacy controls.

  • No training data usage
  • Data isolation by default
  • User-controlled data sharing

Secure Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade infrastructure with multi-layered security, continuous monitoring, and automated threat detection systems.

  • 24/7 security monitoring
  • Automated threat detection
  • Multi-region redundancy
